OhSint Machine

This is a walkthrough for the Osint Machine from TryHackMe . They just give us an image. Let’s open the image, we get the image of WindowsXp. Let’s run Exiftool to get more information on the image. Here is the command to install it.

We get this information from Exiftool. From this we get the name of the image Owner, the person’s name is OWoodflint.
Let’s do a quick Google Search. Search: “OWoodflint” with the quotes on Google.
We get three webpages which are useful for us. Twitter, Github and A blog Website.
Let’s open Twitter, We get the user’s avatar is a picture of a Cat. Scroll down to the tweets of a person . We get a BSSID of the person’s WIFI. Let’s go to this website to find more information on the location and SSID. Login to the website[I personally suggest you to use a temporary email address].
Under ‘View’, you go to ‘Advanced Search’ and enter the BSSID from the tweet and click on search. Wait for a minute, scroll down and click on the map option next to the scan. We get the city is London. Next to that we also get the SSID[name of the WIFI Network].It is called UnileverWifi
We don’t get more information from Twitter, Let’s visit the Github Page. We get the personal email-address of OWoodflint. The Email-Address is: OWoodflint@gmail.com , The platform is: Github .

Now let’s go to the blogpage.

This tells us that he is in New York,so he might be on vacation in New York.The line after that looks like the password, so just paste that in the TryHackMe question, we get it correct.
